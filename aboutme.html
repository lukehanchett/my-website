About Stephen Curry

Stephen Curry, also known as "Steph," is widely regarded as the greatest shooter in NBA history. Born on March 14, 1988, in Akron, Ohio, he played college basketball at Davidson College where he rose to national prominence during the NCAA Tournament.

Drafted 7th overall by the Golden State Warriors in 2009, Steph transformed the game with his incredible range, quick release, and elite ball handling. He's a 2-time NBA MVP, 4-time NBA Champion, and holds countless three-point shooting records. Off the court, he's a philanthropist, entrepreneur, and family man who continues to inspire fans around the world.
