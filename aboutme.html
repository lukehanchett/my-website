All About Nikola Jokić

Nikola Jokić is a Serbian basketball player known for his incredible passing, court vision, and versatility. He plays for the Denver Nuggets and is one of the best centers in the game.

Fun Facts

  • Loves horses and owns a stable in Serbia
  • Known for triple-doubles as a center
  • Drafted 41st overall in 2014
  1. Born in Sombor, Serbia
  2. NBA MVP (2021, 2022)
  3. NBA Champion (2023)
  4. Has a calm and humble personality
  5. Enjoys spending time with family and horses
